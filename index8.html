Believer's Club

Welcome to the world of the “Believer’s Club” - a story about a D&D group where Max, the DM, gets into a forbidden relationship with one of the players. To keep the relationship a secret, the rules of the game are bent to the point of breaking - and with one slip up, the group could dissolve entirely! As the covert romance pulls at the seams of their friendship, can Max remain an impartial adjudicator of the game world, or will his forbidden love get the better of him?
