About Me

I'm Shay Inkpen, Temple University 2024, Film and Media Arts. You can find my scripts, short stories, and essays here, and my video projects here. If you're interested in collaborating on a project or starting something new, feel free to reach out! I'd love to connect. (Photo by Victoria Wilcox, @torian.studios)
I spent time gaffing for an independent film company in the Poconos, helping to create the look and feel of "Genesis Valley". We worked day and night to properly illuminate the slow-burn, post-apocalyptic thriller. (BTS by Rebecca Judd, @rebeccajuddphoto).
I provided script supervision for the film Marina, starring Peter Friedman ("Succession") and Grace McLean. An incredible honor to work with such talented actors and crewmembers. (BTS by Victoria Wilcox, @torian.studios).
I had the rare privilege of having my script read at the Phoenixville Film Festival. It was a great experience to meet new people, hear new stories, and experience the storytelling techniques others use to navigate their worlds. Looking forward to next year!
